Why Choose VoltDrive?

Extended Range

Up to 450 miles on a single charge. Our advanced battery technology ensures you can go further than ever before without worrying about running out of power. The proprietary cell chemistry developed by our engineers delivers industry-leading energy density.

Fast Charging

0-80% in just 18 minutes with our Supercharger network. We've invested billions in building the most comprehensive fast-charging infrastructure across North America, Europe, and Asia. Never wait long to get back on the road.

Autonomous Driving

Level 3 autonomy comes standard on all models. Our AI-powered driving system uses 12 cameras, 5 radar units, and ultrasonic sensors to navigate complex traffic situations. Over-the-air updates continuously improve capabilities.

Zero Emissions

100% electric means zero tailpipe emissions. Join the movement toward sustainable transportation and reduce your carbon footprint. Every VoltDrive on the road displaces approximately 4.6 metric tons of CO2 annually compared to gas vehicles.
