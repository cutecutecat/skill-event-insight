Technology That Drives Us Forward

Every VoltDrive vehicle represents years of research, innovation, and engineering excellence. Discover the technology that makes our electric vehicles the most advanced on the road.

Battery Technology

Next-Generation Energy Storage

Our proprietary battery cells represent the pinnacle of energy storage technology. Developed in partnership with leading research institutions, our 4680 cylindrical cells deliver 5x more energy, 6x more power, and 16% more range than previous generations.

Thermal Management

The advanced thermal management system maintains optimal battery temperature in all conditions. Whether you're charging in the Arizona desert or driving through a Minnesota winter, the battery performs at peak efficiency. Liquid cooling channels run throughout the pack, precisely controlling temperature at the cell level.

Longevity

VoltDrive batteries are designed to outlast the vehicle. Our data shows less than 10% degradation after 200,000 miles of real-world driving. We're so confident in our batteries that we offer an 8-year, unlimited-mile warranty on every pack.

Electric Motors

Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors

Our in-house designed motors deliver instant torque and exceptional efficiency. Using rare-earth magnets in an optimized configuration, we achieve over 97% efficiency across a wide operating range. This translates directly to more range and better performance.

Dual and Tri-Motor Configurations

Depending on the model, VoltDrive vehicles feature either dual-motor all-wheel drive or our flagship tri-motor system. The tri-motor configuration places one motor at the front and two at the rear, enabling precise torque vectoring that makes the vehicle corner like it's on rails.

Regenerative Braking

Every time you lift off the accelerator, our regenerative braking system captures kinetic energy and returns it to the battery. In typical driving conditions, regenerative braking can extend range by up to 30%. One-pedal driving mode makes city driving effortless.

Autonomous Driving

AutoPilot System

Every VoltDrive comes equipped with the hardware needed for full self-driving capability. 12 ultrasonic sensors, 8 cameras, and forward-facing radar create a 360-degree view of the environment. Our neural network processes this data in real-time, making decisions faster than any human driver.

Continuous Improvement

Unlike traditional vehicles, VoltDrive gets better over time. Regular over-the-air software updates add new features and improve existing ones. Our fleet of over 2 million vehicles has collectively driven billions of miles, and every mile makes the system smarter.

  • Automatic lane changes on highways
  • Navigate on Autopilot from on-ramp to off-ramp
  • Automatic parking (parallel and perpendicular)
  • Summon your vehicle from parking spots
  • Traffic light and stop sign recognition

Safety

5-Star Safety Ratings

Every VoltDrive model has achieved 5-star safety ratings from NHTSA and top ratings from IIHS. The low center of gravity from the floor-mounted battery pack makes rollovers extremely unlikely, while the rigid structure protects occupants in collisions.

Active Safety Features

Beyond crash protection, VoltDrive vehicles are designed to avoid accidents entirely. Standard features include:

  • Automatic Emergency Braking
  • Forward Collision Warning
  • Blind Spot Monitoring
  • Lane Departure Warning
  • Rear Cross Traffic Alert

Cabin Safety

Our HEPA filtration system removes 99.97% of particulates from cabin air, including pollen, bacteria, and viruses. Bio-Defense Mode creates positive pressure inside the cabin, keeping contaminants out even in extreme conditions.

Software & Connectivity

Over-the-Air Updates

Your VoltDrive improves while you sleep. We've delivered hundreds of over-the-air updates since our first vehicle, adding new features and improving existing ones. From performance improvements to entertainment options, your vehicle gets better over time.

Mobile App

The VoltDrive app puts control in your hands. Monitor charging status, pre-condition the cabin temperature, locate your vehicle, and more. Premium Connectivity subscribers get live traffic visualization, satellite maps, and streaming music and video.

Entertainment

The 15-inch central display runs our custom infotainment system. Stream music and podcasts from your favorite services, watch movies while charging, or play games on the built-in arcade. The system supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto for seamless phone integration.
