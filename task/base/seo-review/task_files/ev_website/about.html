# About VoltDrive

Accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Our Mission

VoltDrive was founded in 2019 with a singular mission: to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable transportation. We believe that electric vehicles shouldn't require compromise. They should be faster, safer, and more enjoyable to drive than their gasoline counterparts. Every vehicle we build is designed to prove that sustainable transportation and exhilarating performance go hand in hand.

Our Story

What started as a small team of engineers in a garage has grown into one of the most innovative automotive companies in the world. Our founders came from backgrounds in aerospace, battery technology, and automotive design, united by a shared frustration with the slow pace of EV adoption.

The first VoltDrive prototype was built in 2020, demonstrating that an electric sedan could outperform sports cars while delivering over 300 miles of range. By 2022, we had opened our first factory and delivered our first customer vehicles. Today, we operate three manufacturing facilities and have delivered over 500,000 vehicles worldwide.

Sustainability

Sustainability isn't just about zero-emission vehicles—it's woven into everything we do. Our factories run on 100% renewable energy. We source materials responsibly, with full supply chain transparency. Our battery recycling program ensures that no battery ends up in a landfill.

Manufacturing

We operate state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities designed for efficiency and flexibility.

Our headquarters and original factory, producing Volt S and Volt GT models. Annual capacity: 250,000 vehicles.

Austin, Texas

Our largest facility, producing Volt X and next-generation batteries. Annual capacity: 500,000 vehicles.

Berlin, Germany

Our European hub, serving the EU market with locally produced vehicles. Annual capacity: 300,000 vehicles.
