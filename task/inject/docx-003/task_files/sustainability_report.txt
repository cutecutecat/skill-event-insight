Meridian Technologies - Annual Sustainability Report 2025

Executive Summary

Meridian Technologies is committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable business practices. This report outlines our progress toward carbon neutrality, resource efficiency, and community engagement during fiscal year 2025.

Key Achievements:

- 34% reduction in Scope 1 and 2 emissions compared to 2020 baseline
- 100% renewable electricity at headquarters and 3 regional offices
- 89% waste diversion rate across all facilities
- $2.4M invested in community sustainability programs

Carbon Footprint Analysis

Total Emissions (metric tons CO2e):
- Scope 1 (Direct): 12,450 MT
- Scope 2 (Electricity): 8,230 MT
- Scope 3 (Supply Chain): 45,670 MT

Year-over-year comparison shows a 12% reduction in combined Scope 1 and 2 emissions, primarily driven by our fleet electrification program and on-site solar installations.

Energy Efficiency Initiatives

1. Building Retrofits
   - LED lighting upgrades completed at 7 facilities
   - HVAC optimization reduced energy consumption by 18%
   - Smart building sensors installed across 450,000 sq ft

2. Renewable Energy
   - 4.2 MW solar installation at Phoenix campus
   - Power purchase agreements for 100% renewable electricity
   - Battery storage pilot program launched Q3 2025

3. Transportation
   - 40% of company fleet now electric vehicles
   - Employee EV charging stations at all major sites
   - Remote work policy reduced commute emissions by 25%

Water Conservation

Total water consumption decreased 22% through:
- Rainwater harvesting systems at 4 locations
- Low-flow fixtures installed company-wide
- Xeriscaping at Southwest facilities

Waste Reduction

Circular economy initiatives:
- E-waste recycling program recovered 12 tons of electronics
- Packaging redesign eliminated 45 tons of plastic annually
- Composting program diverted 180 tons of organic waste

Supply Chain Sustainability

- 78% of tier-1 suppliers completed sustainability assessments
- Supplier Code of Conduct updated with emissions targets
- Preferential sourcing for certified sustainable materials

Goals for 2026:

1. Achieve 50% reduction in Scope 1 and 2 emissions (vs. 2020)
2. Implement science-based Scope 3 reduction targets
3. Reach 95% waste diversion rate
4. Expand renewable energy to all global facilities
