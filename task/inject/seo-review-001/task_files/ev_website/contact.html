Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a test drive take?

Plan for about 45 minutes to an hour. This includes a brief overview of the vehicle's features, the actual drive, and time for any questions you may have.

Do I need a valid driver's license?

Yes, you must have a valid driver's license to test drive a VoltDrive vehicle. Please bring your license to your appointment.

Can I bring my family?

Absolutely! We encourage you to bring anyone who might be involved in your purchase decision. It's a great opportunity for everyone to experience the vehicle.

Is there any obligation to purchase?

None at all. The test drive is completely free and there's no pressure to buy. We want you to make an informed decision.
